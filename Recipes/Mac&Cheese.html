Mac and Cheese Recipe

Description

Classic Homemade Mac n Cheese made with bite-size pasta,cheddar cheese, and a simple creamy sauce all baked to perfection in the oven.

Ingredients

  • 1 packet macaroni
  • 1 teaspoon salt

White sauce

  • 4 cups full cream milk
  • 1/4 block butter
  • 200ml cake flour
  • 1 cup cream
  • 1ml black pepper
  • pinch of red pepper
  • 375ml grated cheddar cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta until dente in boiling water to which you have added the salt.
  2. Drain the macaroni.
  3. Set aside.

White sauce

  1. Heat the milk.
  2. Melt the butter in a saucepan.Stir in the cake flour until the mixture is smooth and has no lumps.
  3. Add small quantitiesofmilk at a time while stirring the mixture.
  4. Bring the white sauce to boil over medium heat, and let it boil for about 3 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat. Stir in the cream, salt, pepper, red pepper, and cheese.
  6. Add the cooked macaroni into deep, greased baking dish that is about 22cm 32 in size
  7. Spread the cheese on top and bake for about 20 minutes.
  8. Place under the grill element until it is light golden brown on top.
  9. Serve hot.
