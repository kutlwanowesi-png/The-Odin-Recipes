Pizza Cups Recipe

Description

Pizza in muffin form, how fun? Stuffed pizza cups with cheese and vegetables, tastes delicious and great for kids and toddlers.

Ingredients

  • 1 batch pizza dough
  • 1 cup pizza sauce or ketchup
  • Finely chopped bell peppers
  • Grated mozarella cheese
  • Salt to taste
  • Olive oil as needed

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 200 degrees C. Grease 12 cup muffin pan with oil and set side.
  2. Take your pizza dough and roll it into a long rectangle.
  3. Cut small circles from it using a cutter. Press it into muffin pan and brush it with oil.
  4. Now pop this in oven and bake for 5 minutes, remove it and if it looks puffy, just use a spoon to flatten it.
  5. Now spread pizza sauce and add all other ingredients into each muffin. Finally spread some cheese and italian seasoning.
  6. Now brush it with some oil in the edges.
  7. Pop it in the oven and bake for 8 to 10 minutes.
  8. Remove it and cool litlle then serve.
